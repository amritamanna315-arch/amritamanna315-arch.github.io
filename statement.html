Artist Statement

My artistic practice explores the relationship between identity, memory, and the human body. Through painting and mixed media, I investigate how personal experiences and physical marks become visual narratives.


My current research focuses on Identity, where birthmarks are considered symbols of individuality rather than imperfections. Each mark represents a unique history, carrying memories that shape personal identity.


Through this ongoing visual exploration, I seek to create works that invite viewers to reflect on the connections between the body, memory, and the stories that define who we are.
