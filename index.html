About Me

I'm Amrita Manna, a contemporary visual artist and Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) student at the Indian College of Arts & Draftsmanship, Kolkata. My artistic practice explores painting through observation, emotion and experimentation across watercolor, oil, mixed media and drawing.
