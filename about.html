About

I am Amrita Manna, a Visual Arts Practitioner and Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) student at The Indian College of Arts & Draftsmanship, Kolkata.


My artistic practice explores themes of memory, identity, and human experience through painting and mixed media. My ongoing research-based project, Core Memory, investigates birthmarks as symbols of identity, personal history, and emotional memory.


I believe that every artwork carries a story, and every mark has the potential to preserve memory beyond words. Through my practice, I aim to create works that encourage reflection on identity, belonging, and lived experience.
